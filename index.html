About Copper Spot

Warm service, generous food and a relaxed North Shore atmosphere.

Copper Spot Cafe & Eatery is a cosy cafe in Albany serving breakfast, brunch, lunch, coffee, desserts and drinks from a bright corner on Corinthian Drive.

The space feels casual, cosy and slightly trendy, with friendly service, good presentation and a menu that suits coffee catch-ups, quick bites, solo dining, family brunches and casual meetings.

FAQ

Quick answers before you visit.

For bookings and the latest menu availability, the best option is to call Copper Spot directly.

Reserve by Phone
Do you take bookings?

Yes. Copper Spot accepts reservations by phone. Please call 09 447 1788 to reserve a table.

What are your kitchen hours?

The kitchen opens at 8:00 am Monday to Friday, 8:30 am Saturday and 9:00 am Sunday. It closes at 2:30 pm Monday to Saturday and 2:00 pm Sunday.

Do you offer takeaway?

Yes. Takeaway is available for coffee, drinks, quick bites and suitable menu items.

Is there outdoor seating?

Yes. Copper Spot has outdoor seating, which is also the dog-friendly area.

Are dogs allowed?

Dogs are allowed outside. Please keep dogs comfortable and considerate of other guests.

Is the cafe wheelchair accessible?

Yes. Copper Spot has a wheelchair-accessible entrance and wheelchair-accessible seating.

Do you have vegetarian or vegan options?

Yes. The menu includes vegetarian and vegan options, along with healthy options and small plates.

Is Copper Spot good for kids?

Yes. The cafe is good for kids and has a kids’ menu.

Is parking available?

Parking options include free street parking, a free parking lot and paid street parking nearby.

Can I work on my laptop there?

Yes. Copper Spot is suitable for working on a laptop, especially for a casual coffee or lunch visit.
